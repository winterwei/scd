Team

Stacey cohen

AFTER GRADUATING FROM THE ESTEEMED INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY OF DESIGN & TECHNOLOGY, PRINCIPAL DESIGNER STACEY COHEN HAS FOCUSED HER CAREER ON DOING WHAT SHE LOVES BEST; WORKING WITH CLIENTS TO GENERATE CREATIVE & FUNCTIONAL SOLUTIONS TO IMPROVE THE QUALITY OF OUR DAILY WORK/LIVE ENVIRONMENTS. AFTER SPENDING TWO YEARS DOING THE DESIGN WORK AND INTERIOR STYLING FOR A HIGH PROFILE CONDOMINIUM DEVELOPER IN TORONTO, STACEY HAD THE VISION AND AMBITION TO DEVELOP HER OWN FIRM, STACEY COHEN DESIGN, IN ORDER TO TRANSFORM THE DESIGN EXPERIENCE FOR HER CLIENTS. THROUGH A REFINED, FULL SERVICE DESIGN EXPERIENCE, STACEY COHEN DESIGN RESPONDS TO OUR CLIENT’S NEEDS, VISION, AND INSPIRATION IN ORDER TO CREATE DESIGN SOLUTIONS THAT PROVIDE MAXIMUM ENJOYMENT AND FUNCTION. A PERSONAL, COLLABORATIVE CONNECTION WITH EACH CLIENT IS HOW WE ENSURE DESIGN COMES TO LIFE. WE THRIVE ON CREATING SPACES THAT WORK; WE LOVE CHALLENGES AND ARE COMMITTED TO PROVIDING A LEVEL OF SERVICE THAT EXCEEDS OUR CLIENTS’ EXPECTATIONS. OUR DESIGNS ARE BUILT TO LAST WITH FUNCTION AT THE FOREFRONT OF EVERY PROJECT.

AS A CLIENT OF IBSC YOU RETAIN ACCESS TO OUR BROAD RANGE OF PROFESSIONAL DESIGN AND CONSULTATIVE SERVICES. REGARDLESS OF THE SIZE OF THE PROJECT, WE MAKE SURE EVERY ELEMENT IS PLANNED, SELECTED AND EXECUTED WITH PRECISION.
