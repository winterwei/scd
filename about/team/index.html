Team

Stacey cohen

After graduating from the esteemed International Academy of Design & Technology, principal designer Stacey Cohen has focused her career on doing what she loves best; working with clients to generate creative and functional solutions to improve the quality of work/live environments. Her ambition to develop her own firm grew over two years of design and interior styling for a high profile developer in Toronto.

Stacey saw her vision through, and SCD was born out of a desire to transform the design experience. As principal designer, Stacey creates all initial design concepts, custom furniture designs, sources materials and finishes and oversees the entire design process for each project.
